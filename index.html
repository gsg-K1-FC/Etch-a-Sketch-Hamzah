How to use?

  • Press (S) or click to start/stop sketching
  • Press (E) or click to start/stop erasing

Coloring System

  • Rainbow Mode:
    • Default Mode
    • Each cell will be colored with randomly picked color when being hovered
    • If you hover over a colored cell, its colore will get 10% darker
  • Color Picker Mode:
    • You can pick the color you want
    • You need to click to choose
    • Once you're here you can get back to Rainbow mode by refreshing the page
